about us

The Bardez Beekeepers Society is an association of hobby and commercial beekeepers from Goa State, registered in 2022. It operates as a distinct legal entity with the primary objective of promoting commercial beekeeping within the state.

The society offers practical training sessions ranging from one to five days, aimed at educating individuals in beekeeping practices. These sessions cover various aspects of beekeeping, leveraging techniques suitable for the Western Ghats region. In addition to training, members have access to standard beekeeping equipment supplied by the society, ensuring that both novice and experienced beekeepers have the necessary tools to maintain their hives effectively.

The society also facilitates the purchase of honey from its members, providing a reliable market for their produce and supporting the commercial aspect of beekeeping. By utilizing Western Ghats beekeeping techniques, the Bardez Beekeepers Society aims to establish a network of 1000 beekeepers managing 5000 beehives. The target is to achieve a production of 100,000 kilograms (one lakh kgs) of honey by the year 2030.

The Bardez Beekeepers Society is dedicated to fostering the growth of the beekeeping industry in Goa, supporting both hobbyists and commercial beekeepers in their endeavors to produce high-quality honey and other bee products.
